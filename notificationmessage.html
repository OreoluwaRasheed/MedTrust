Update on fake drug

Update in january 2022, counterfeit drugs remain a significant global issue particularly in regions with weak regulatory oversight and enforcement.

Pharmaceutical companies and government are increasingly adopting technology-driven solutions such as blockchain, RFID tags, and unique sterilization codes to track and authenticate drugs throughout the supply chain.

Organizations like wolrd Health Organization (WHO), interpol, and the pharmaceutical Security Institute (PSI) continue to work collaboratively with governments,law enforcement agencies, and industry stakeholders to combat the production and distribution of fake drugs

Please report any suspected instances of fake drugs to the appropriate authorities for investigation and action
